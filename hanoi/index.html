The Great Hanoi Rat Hunt

Game and Graphics by Jack Engle, Jiarong Ding
0 cents 0 rats Day: 1

Controls:
Arrow Keys or WASD to move character in cardinal directions, Shift to interact, M to enable (and disable) the music.

Original graphic history by Professor Michael G. Vann. Pokemon Red Route 3 Theme Music by Junichi Masuda. All rights reserved.

Hints:

- At the west old quarter area there's a Vietnamese man who, starting Day 5 will give you a hint to use the breeder
- The rat breeder is to the north of the old quarter
- In the very southeast, starting day 5 a french guy will discourage you from talking to the rat supplier on the coastline
- On day 15 the days stop progressing and to end the game you talk to the rat buyer infront of the big palace
- Most other characters have their dialogue change every several days and will talk about things like the Bubonic plague, Dr. Yersin, the exposition, the rat hunt, their family, etc.
